Our process is one of the things that makes us very competitive in the pest control market.

1

INSPECTION, PREVENTIVE ACTION, IDENTIFICATION

Our regular inspections concentrate on the locations where pests are most likely to develop, such as receiving docks, storage spaces, staff break rooms, the locations of recent ingredient spills, etc., and we look for any potential entrance points, food and water supplies that can increase insect infestations.Various pests exhibit varying behavior. We make sure pests can be eradicated more effectively and with less danger of harming other organisms by determining the troublesome species.

2

ANALYSIS, TREATMENT SELECTION

Once the pest has been correctly identified, we determine why they reside in your facility. We analyze the local area for debris, moisture accumulation, odors, infestations etc,.The responses to these questions will lead us to the best choice of control techniques.The "correct treatment" will frequently include a mix of actions, including chemical treatments, baiting, and trapping. But by focusing on non-chemical methods first, we can ensure that our pest control program is efficiently reducing pests at the least risk to your food safety program, non-target species and the environment.

3

MONITORING, DOCUMENTATION

We are constantly monitoring your facility for pest activity and operational changes which can protect against infestation and help eliminate existing ones. Our pest management professional will most likely visit your facility on a bi-weekly, weekly or monthly basis. Not only so, but we keep up-to-date pest control documentation of our visits to facilitate future pest control services and meet business standards.
